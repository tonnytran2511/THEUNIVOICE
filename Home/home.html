ABOUT THE UNIVOICE

The UNIVoice - a professional and meaningful platform for university students in Ho Chi Minh City – aims to empower young people to express themselves confidently, think critically, and become dynamic voices of the new generation.

With the spirit of growth and connection, The UNIVoice not only celebrates promising MC talents but also nurtures a new wave of students who are articulate, bold, and full of passion. It embodies the university’s commitment to holistic education – fostering confident, humanistic, and innovative young individuals ready to make a positive impact on society.
